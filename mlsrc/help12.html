ML-12 — Triangle Solution (2)

Continuation of ML-11, Triangle Solution (1). This program will work out various parameters of a triangle given some of them: given either two angles and the included side (ASA) or two angles and a non-included side (SAA), it will compute the remaining angles and sides. It can also work out the area of a triangle once a computation has been done.

Knowing ASA

First, enter the known angles and side of the triangle, in any order:

  • Enter included side a: a  A 
  • Enter angle B: B  B 
  • Enter angle C: C  C 

Then, compute angle A: 2nd A´

Now, you can obtain the remaining sides, in either order:

  • side b: D
  • side c: E

Knowing SAA

First, enter the known angles and side, in any order:

  • Enter non-included side a: a  A 
  • Enter angle A: A  B 
  • Enter angle C: C  C 

Then, compute angle B: 2nd B´

Now, you can obtain the remaining sides, in either order:

  • side b: D
  • side c: E

Triangle Area

Having done any of the previous computations, simply press 2nd C´ to determine the area of the triangle.

This can also be done after any of the computations in ML-11, Triangle Solutions (1).

Formulas Used

A + B + C = 180° or 2π

c2 = a2 + b2 - 2ab cos C

a ÷ sin A = b ÷ sin B = c ÷ sin C

Area = sqrt(s(s - a)(s - b)(s - c)) where s = (a + b + c) ÷ 2.

Registers used: 01-07.
